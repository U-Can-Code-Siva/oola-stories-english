# MyFirstStory

**Genre:** Fantasy
**Language:** English

## Theme
A journey into a dream world with a little princess

## Initial Plot
A little princess gets teleported into a new different world.  The story describes the moments when she finds her favourite things, getting treated royally, pampered, etc.  The story is a very colourful one giving all the sense of happiness.
